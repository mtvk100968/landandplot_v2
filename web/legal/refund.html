Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 22 July 2025

This policy explains how you can cancel or seek a refund for any paid service purchased through LANDANDPLOT (“Platform”), operated by LUMPSUMIFY PVT LTD.

1. Scope

  • The only paid service currently offered is posting a property listing on the Platform.

2. Cancellations

  • You may request cancellation within 24 hours of purchasing the paid service, provided service delivery has not started (e.g., your listing has not gone live).
  • If the listing is already published or processing has begun, cancellation may not be accepted and the fee may not be refundable.

3. Complaints / Service Issues

  • If you believe the delivered service is defective or not as described, contact us within 7 days of service activation. We will review and decide on an appropriate resolution (correction, re‑provision, partial/full refund, or credit).

4. Refunds

  • Once a refund is approved by LANDANDPLOT, it may take up to 30 days to process the refund to your original payment method or in‑app wallet (as applicable).
  • No special non‑refundable charges apply at this time.

5. How to Request a Cancellation / Refund

  • Email: admin@landandplot.com
  • Include: order/payment ID, registered phone/email, and a brief reason.
  • Our support team will reply with next steps and status updates.
